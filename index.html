About Me!

As a software developer, I am driven by an insatiable curiosity and a relentless desire to build complex ideas using creative solutions. With a foundation in production stage management in the arts, I've had the privilege of acquiring my MSCS at Northeastern University, which has cultivated my technical skills and deepened my passion for problem-solving through code.

What sets me apart is my unwavering enthusiasm for learning. In the ever-changing landscape of technology, I thrive on challenges that require me to adapt and grow. My commitment to continuous improvement is not only a personal ethos but a driving force that fuels my professional journey.
